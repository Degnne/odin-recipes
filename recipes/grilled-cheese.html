Grilled Cheese

Description

A warm, gooey, and cheesey sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 slice of American cheese
  • Mayonaise

Steps

  1. Spread mayonaise on one half of each slice of bread.
  2. Place one piece of bread in a hot pan, mayo side down.
  3. Place cheese on bread, followed by the other slice of bread, mayo side up.
  4. Cook until each side is golden brown, flipping halfway through.
