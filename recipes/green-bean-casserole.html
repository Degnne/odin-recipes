Green Bean Casserole

Description

A creamy side dish made from green beans and cream of mushroom soup and topped with crispy onions.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of green beans
  • 1 can of cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 Tbsp of soy sauce
  • 1 cup of fried onions, divided

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Grease baking dish.
  3. Combine green beans, soup, milk, soy sauce, and half of the onions in the baking dish.
  4. Top with remaining onions.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes.
